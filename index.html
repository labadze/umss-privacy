2) Why we request Android permissions

  • Camera — android.permission.CAMERA: Capture photos/videos you choose to submit (no background capture).
  • Microphone — android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO: Record audio with video/voice input (no background recording).
  • Location — ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION: Location‑based municipal services/maps. Not tracked in background unless required and granted.
  • Notifications — POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+): Verification codes and service alerts.
  • Network & power — INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE, CHANGE_NETWORK_STATE, WAKE_LOCK, MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS: Connectivity and reliability.
  • Bluetooth (legacy) — BLUETOOTH, BLUETOOTH_ADMIN: Optional accessory/municipal device connections (not for tracking).
  • SMS (auto‑fill) — RECEIVE_SMS, READ_SMS: If enabled, used only to auto‑fill one‑time codes. We do not read/store other SMS content. We plan to use Android’s SMS Retriever / User Consent APIs that do not require SMS permissions.
  • Features — android.hardware.camera, android.hardware.camera.autofocus: Declare camera capability.

3) How we use your data
